Background

I grew up in Székelyudvarhely (map). I am an ethnic Hungarian from Romania. My name is pronounced "Veresh Pay-ter", last name first, first name second. I got my MSc from the Eötvös University in Budapest. I spent a year in Cork, Ireland. I got my PhD in 2011. Currently I am a postdoc at CSPAR at UAH in Alabama, I formerly worked at GWU and at Penn State, USA (map).
In my spare time I enjoy playing basketball and reading.
